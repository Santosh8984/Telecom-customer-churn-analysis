# Telecom-customer-churn-analysis
📊 Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing telecom customer churn through KPI tracking, customer segmentation, revenue impact analysis, and actionable business insights.

Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the biggest challenges for telecom companies. Losing customers not only reduces revenue but also increases customer acquisition costs. This project uses Power BI to analyze customer churn, uncover the factors driving customer attrition, and provide actionable insights that support better retention strategies.

The dashboard transforms raw customer data into meaningful business insights, helping decision-makers identify high-risk customer segments and estimate the financial impact of churn.

🎯 Business Problem

The company wanted to understand why customers were leaving and which customer groups required immediate attention. This dashboard answers several key business questions, including:

Why are customers leaving the company?
Which contract type has the highest churn rate?
Which customer segments are most likely to churn?
How much monthly revenue is at risk because of churn?
Which services are associated with higher customer retention?
📂 Dataset Information

The analysis is based on a telecom customer dataset containing 7,043 customer records and includes the following information:

Customer Demographics
Contract Type
Internet Service
Monthly Charges
Payment Method
Online Security
Customer Tenure
Churn Status
📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the most important business metrics:

Overall Churn Rate (%)
Total Customers Lost
Average Tenure of Churned Customers
Average Monthly Charges (Churned vs. Retained)
Revenue at Risk ($)
Churn Rate by Contract Type

💡 Key Business Insights

The analysis revealed several important findings:

Customers with Month-to-Month contracts have the highest likelihood of churning.
Customers with shorter tenure are significantly more likely to leave the company.
Customers without Online Security show considerably higher churn rates.
Monthly revenue at risk can be estimated using churned customers' monthly charges.
Payment methods also influence customer churn behaviour.
